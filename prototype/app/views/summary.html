Find finance options for your businesss

The options you have selected will inform which finance options are available to your business. Click 'Find finance' to see the relevant opportunities, or edit your choices below.

Before applying for additional funding, you should consider:

  • How will you spend the money?
  • How will upfront costs, such as employing more staff or setting up new premises, affect your cash flow?
  • How soon do you need the money?
  • If you’re a sole trader, what assets – like a car or a house – do you have to use as a guarantee?

Talking to a professional adviser can help you to work out whether it makes financial sense to take on loans or investment. You can find a chartered accountant on the Institute of Chartered Accountants (ICAEW) website, or a solicitor on the Law Society website.
