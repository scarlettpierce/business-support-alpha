Business Growth: Step 1 of 5 What type of support do you need?

Funding options

There are thousands of funding schemes across the UK that can help with the costs of growing your business. Each finance option has benefits and costs attached.

  • Equity means that an organisation will own part of your business in exchange for money.
  • A loan is a sum of money that you can borrow and then will be required to pay back, often with interest.
  • A grant is a sum of money that you are given and you do not have to pay back, but there may be some conditions on how you can use it. Grants usually have a particular objective, like supporting research and development or creating jobs.
